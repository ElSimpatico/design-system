## Font styles

For font styles, the free <a href="https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Montserrat">Monserrat Google Fonts</a> are used.

Monserrate Google Font has five font styles that graduate evenly in terms of weight. We currently use:

-   RegularFont: `MontserratRegular`, sans-serif;
-   SemiBoldFont: `MontserratSemiBold`, sans-serif;
